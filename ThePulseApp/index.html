FAQ

What types of products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of products, including clothing (t-shirts, hoodies, jackets), accessories (hats, belts, jewelry), posters, vinyl records, and other merchandise related to metal and rock bands.

Are the products original and licensed?

Yes, all our products are original and licensed. We work directly with bands and official suppliers to ensure the highest quality and authenticity. you can always check which bands we work with in Bands/Artists.

How can I place an order and what are the payment methods?

Orders can be placed directly on our website. We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

What is the delivery time and shipping costs?

The standard delivery time is usually between 3 to 7 business days, depending on the delivery location. Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of the order and the delivery address, and will be displayed in the cart before checkout. We also offer an Extra Delivery option for an additional fee, which ensures delivery within 1 business day.

Can I return or exchange the ordered products?

Yes, we accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of the purchase date, provided that the products are in their original condition with the original tags. To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service department.
